Frequently Asked Questions

What should I prepare for my consultation?
Come with inspiration images, preferred colors, and details about your event. For virtual consultations, ensure good lighting and a stable internet connection.
How long does a bespoke piece take to create?
Bespoke pieces typically take 6-12 weeks depending on complexity and embellishments. Hand-beaded designs may require additional time. We recommend booking at least 3 months before your event.
What is your payment structure?
We require a 50% deposit to begin work on your piece, with the balance due upon completion and before delivery. All major payment methods are accepted.
Can I make changes after the design is finalized?
Minor adjustments can be accommodated during fitting sessions. Major design changes may incur additional fees and extend the timeline. We recommend finalizing all design decisions during the consultation.
